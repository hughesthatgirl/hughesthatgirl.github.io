About Me

I’m a self-taught front end developer who’s spent most of my career creating and coding design systems from scratch for a variety of different businesses. This has given me a solid, practical understanding of how to build systems and interfaces that truly work for the intended user.


Accessibility first is my personal philosophy for design and the web—and I believe it should be a top priority for all developers. An accessible web builds a more equitable society for everyone.


While I am deeply passionate about front end development, I am ready to take on a new challenge which is why I've decided to broaden my skill set and hope to move towards a more full-stack role.
